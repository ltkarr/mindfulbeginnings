Accommodations / notes
A great deal of information is presented in a short period of time during the Safe Sitter® course. We want every child to succeed, and we will work with you to make alternate plans if your child has difficulty keeping up. Please let us know if there is anything we should know to help your child succeed. If your child needs accommodations, please let the Instructor or Site Coordinator know as soon as possible. Please note: Safe Sitter® does not provide CPR certification, release the names of graduates, or act as a referral source of babysitters.
